🏕️ Campers Info 🏕️

You're one of our special camping guests! Here's everything you need to know for the full weekend experience.

⏰ Campers Schedule Leading up to wedding

  • Friday (14th) Morning: You're welcome to start arriving the day before the wedding
  • Friday 6PM: Camp Dinner
  • Saturday 9AM: Camp Breakfast
  • Saturday all day: Help us setup the wholeass wedding! Snacks & Beverages provided
  • Sunday 11AM: Cleanup & Checkout

🍳 Meals Provided

  • Friday dinner
  • Snacks all day
  • Saturday breakfast

Each site has a grill, feel free to bring anything you like to toss on!

🎒 Accomodations and What to bring

  • Everyone will be assigned a cabin/treehouse
  • Cabins are rustic, some have AC/power, others are simple bunk houses
  • Things to bring:
  • Flashlight/headlamp
  • Pillow & towel
  • Layers for cool nights
  • Camp chair (optional)
  • Bug spray & sunscreen
  • RSVP form will ask if you have any sheets to contribute as linens are not provided

🐕 Dog Policy

While we obviously love dogs, dog attendees will be limited to avoid a dog takeover of the ceremony

  • Please reach out if you would like to bring your canine companion

🔥 Activities

  • Campfire & grilling
  • Stargazing
  • Morning hike (optional)
  • Local bike trails (gravel+mountain)
  • Lawn games

ℹ️ Good to Know

  • Bathhouses on site, think state campground
  • No RV hookups
  • Cell service is limited but not zero
  • Quiet hours after 11PM
